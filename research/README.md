proofs. Two tiers:

| File | What it distills |

## `raw/` — the unabridged record

The full gate logs (`gates.md`), the audits (K1 statement, K2 axiom, K3
vacuity, K4 simulated-external, the T7 stack audit), the superseded
rev-11 spec (`Spec.md`) and its proof inventory
(`proof-inventory-rev11.md`), the TLA+ findings, per-topic literature
reports with their verification passes, and the historical process docs
(`BRIEF.md`, `TASKS.md`, `DELIVERY.md`, `RESUME.md`,
`ROADMAP-STATUS.md`). Nothing here is required reading; it exists so
every claim in `processed/` has a paper trail.
